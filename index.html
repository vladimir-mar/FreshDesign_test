TowerIQ, Inc. works towards a safer future by manufacturing reliable, affordable, and easyto-install wireless infrastructure for buildings. Our Tactical Series and UniDAS platforms provide required emergency responder communications. Using simple solutions to complex problems, TowerIQ provides "Signal Where it Matters."
